Our Mission

Connecting Korean Students
Across Ohio

KSA Connect is an annual outdoor conference uniting Korean Student Associations and Korean American Student Associations from universities across Ohio. The 2026 event is proudly hosted by The Ohio State University Korean Student Association, with CWRU KASA, KSA, and UC KSA joining as co-attending organizations.

Whether you're a general member or an executive board member, KSA Connect is your opportunity to celebrate Korean culture and tradition, sharpen your professional and leadership skills, and forge meaningful relationships with fellow passionate students from CWRU, OSU, and UC.

Together, we build a stronger, more unified Korean student community — not just on our own campuses, but across the entire state of Ohio.
